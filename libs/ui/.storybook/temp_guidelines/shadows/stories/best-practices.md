- Consistency:
  Maintain consistent shadow styles throughout the application to ensure a cohesive look and feel.
- Subtlety:
  Use soft, subtle shadows to avoid overwhelming the user. Harsh shadows can be distracting and reduce readability.
- Light Source:
  Define a consistent light source direction to make shadows look natural and coherent.
- Accessibility:
  Ensure shadows do not compromise accessibility. High contrast and clear differentiation between elements are crucial.
- Performance:
  Be mindful of performance impacts. Excessive use of shadows can slow down rendering, especially on lower-end devices.
